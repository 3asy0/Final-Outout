About Us

The 1128 Tea & Cafe - Currimao Branch is a beloved establishment located in Currimao, Ilocos Norte. It has become a favorite spot for both locals and visitors, earning a reputation for serving some of the best beverages in town. The cafe is known for its commitment to quality and affordability, making it a popular choice for those who want to enjoy a delicious drink without breaking the bank. They offer a wide range of beverages, ensuring there’s something for everyone. In addition to their physical location, they also provide delivery services, allowing customers to enjoy their favorite drinks from the comfort of their own homes. The 1128 Tea & Cafe - Currimao Branch is more than just a cafe - it’s a place where people come to relax, catch up with friends, and enjoy a moment of tranquility in their busy lives. Whether you’re a fan of milk tea, coffee, or frappe, you’re sure to find something you love at this charming establishment.
